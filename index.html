Who am I and what do I do?

I'm in the 2015 DevBootcamp cohort (Grasshoppers 2015!) and I'll be spending the next few months learning the world of web development. Previously I've been a localization project manager, instructional designer, eLearning developer, elementary school reading coach, floral designer and jewelry maker.

I'm a huge language nerd and I love learning about the nuances in languages and conveying meaning. My goal is to work within all these fields at once--bringing together a full force of instructional design, web development, languages and culture.

This web page is my first project in DevBootcamp! Its design and structure are still in progress, so I would love any feedback or ideas you may have to improve it. Also, take a look at my blog posts if you get a chance!
