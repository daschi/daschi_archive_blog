Ruby Classes and JavaScript Constructor Functions

2015-06-12

ODE TO RUBY

Ruby Classes, oh how I miss thee,

Your lithe definition so brisk and limber

Tell me all your accessors!

For I must know when the sun is set,

Or if simply a sun to get...

Let me initialize you to the wonders of the world

Each instance you will become a new....instance

Inheriting everything, and likely overriding some

Ride on! Ride on Ruby! Don't be dismayed for you

are loved!

Loved, perhaps, dare I say, more than

These so-called JavaScript constructors or functions or what have you

What do they, pray tell, know about creating new instances?

About setting and getting?

About instance methods?

....everything, you say? They know everything?

Well, we'll see about that!
